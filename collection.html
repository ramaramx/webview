ABOUT

Pride N Joy is a fashion brand based in Bandung, Indonesia, that focused on raw denim, established in 2016. We see raw denim as an art. That's why we always try to produce articles that focused on the finest fabric and materials, highly attention to detail, and low priced.
But it's not only about the products that we care. We are also making some innovations to bring fresh and different shopping experience. FAST PANTS is one of the features that we have the pride on. We also have FREE REPAIR feature that allowed all of our consumer to repair their denim anytime they need.
We will never stop to developing best products and innovation until... we don't know, maybe when the world stop spinning?
