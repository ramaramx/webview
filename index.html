The Story
Somewhere on Earth, there is a majestic city in the middle of a giant desert, named Theopolis. The people have beliefs on 5 Lords; Taj, Nephthys, Nubia, Sydna, and Khamun.
Each article has its own story and character Taj is 15 oz black on black selvedge denim in red-line illustrated by The Lord among Lords. Nephthys is 14 oz blue indigo selvedge denim in gold-line illustrated as The Goddess of Lust.
Nubia is 11 oz blue indigo denim selvedge in red-line illustrated as the Lord of Death. Sydna is 14 oz medium indigo denim selvedge in red-line illustrated as The Guardian of The Underworld Gate. Khamun is 15 oz deep indigo in red-line illustrated as The Tomb King.
